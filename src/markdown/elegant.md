# 🛍️ Elegant Store

**Elegant Store** is a modern e-commerce platform designed to provide a seamless and enjoyable shopping experience.  
With an intuitive interface and secure payment integration, users can easily explore products, add favorites, and make purchases with confidence.  

## ✨ Features

- 🛒 **Shopping Cart** – Add, update, and manage products before checkout.  
- ❤️ **Favorites** – Save items you love for quick access later.  
- ⭐ **Product Reviews** – Read and share reviews to make informed decisions.  
- 💳 **Secure Payments** – Powered by **Stripe** for safe and reliable transactions.  
- 🎨 **User-Friendly Design** – Clean and elegant UI for a smooth browsing experience.  

## 🔐 Why Elegant Store?

- Safe & secure payment gateway  
- Simple and responsive design  
- Personalized shopping with favorites & reviews  
- Fast and efficient checkout process
